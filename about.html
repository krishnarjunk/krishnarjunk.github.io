My Background

My academic journey began at the Central University of Tamil Nadu, where I completed my undergraduate studies in Mathematics. I went on to pursue a Ph.D. at the Harish-Chandra Research Institute (HRI), followed by a postdoctoral fellowship at the Beijing Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Applications (BIMSA).

My Job

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at SRM University, located in the beautiful city of Amaravati, where I enjoy teaching diverse courses and sharing my passion for number theory with students. My research continues to explore deep problems in analytic number theory, especially those connected to modular forms and arithmetic distributions.
